Our Story

From Manila streets to Nueva Ecija fields, our story is simple: curiosity turned into friendship, friendship became love, and now we say "yes" to forever.

  • How We Met

    September 28, 2018

    We met through a mutual friend during a weekday night out — two very different backgrounds but an immediate connection. Conversations that night lasted for hours and kept coming back.

  • In a relationship

    January 07, 2019

    Our first proper date was at Chiquito’s, located near UST. We shared good food, lots of laughter, and stories about our college days. Between bites and smiles, we realized how easy it was to talk and be ourselves around each other.

  • The Proposal

    June 15, 2024

    Dave proposed to her at her favorite restaurant on her birthday. It was an intimate, unforgettable moment — just the two of us, surrounded by good food, warm lights, and the feeling that everything was falling perfectly into place.
